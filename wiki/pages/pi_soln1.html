Plasmid Isolation solution I preparation


For 100 mL, pH 8,

  1. Add the following to an empty Erlenmeyer flask:
    • 0.908 g glucose
    • 0.30283 g Tris
    • 0.40947 g EDTA
  2. Add 10 mL distilled water and mix well.
  3. Maintain pH at 8 using HCl.
  4. Make up the volume to 100 mL.
