Dr. Preeti Srivastava


Primary Investigator

Dr. Preeti Srivastava is a professor at IIT Delhi and the project supervisor for iGEM-IIT Delhi. Prof. Preeti has guided the team from the start, right from the ideation of the project to its execution. Dr. D. Sundar


Secondary Investigator

Dr. D. Sundar is the Institute Chair Professor and the Head of the Department of Biochemical Engineering and Biotechnology at IIT Delhi. He has completed his post-doctoral research from John Hopkins University, Maryland, USA, with his research interests lying in the field of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology. Dr. Sundar has conducted extensive research in the fields of genome editing tool designing, biological studies of natural drugs, and metabolic engineering, reporting impactful insights, and accomplishing multiple awards and accolades for the same. Prof. Sundar has always been a stalwart support for the team, helping them out at every step, and helping them tackle obstacles, always guiding the team towards the right path.
